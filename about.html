How ARC Laundry works. View the FAQ below.

For Laundromat Services
-Visit the website and head to the Laundromat section.
-Select from the categories and press Book button.
-Schedule date and time.
-Press Next button.
-Enter your contact information.
-Review your booking.
-Checkout by pressing Book Now button.

What happens next?

You’ll get a confirmation email and a text message with your booking details. If scheduled for a different day, you will receive a text message a day before the time scheduled. Once the pickup person arrives, you will be required to hand them your laundry load. Once your laundry load arrives at our Laundromat, a picture of its weight and invoice of service shall be sent to you. You chill, We wash, fold, iron and return on the Same Day.

Payment

Payment is made directly to the company via Lipa na M-Pesa.

N/B:

For cancellation of service, a cancellation fee of KES 200 will be required from you for logistic purposes.
